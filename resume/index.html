About

William Woodruff aka Bill or Zill, is a full stack JS and XR engineer with a JSNSD (JS Node Services Developer) cert from the Linux Foundation. Bill prefers using hapipal and hapi on the back-end, and react or react-native on the front-end, but has experience with a variety of tech stacks. Most recently for work projects Bill has been implementing an awesome XR UI at, ex: https://app.alakazam.io/yjnqZq5/vibes! He lead the implementation work on the lobby and in-room UI at Alakazam! Outside of work he's been having lots of fun working on his new chatbot, Chappy! Say hello at https://chappyai.com! Also checkout my personal ShaderFes over at https://zillslab.com!
